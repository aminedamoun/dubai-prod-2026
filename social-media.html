AI-Powered Social Media Marketing

Smarter Social Media Strategy with AI

At Dubai Prod, our social media marketing starts with structure. We design content ecosystems that align brand storytelling, audience psychology, and platform algorithms. Using AI-driven tools like Perplexity, NotebookLM, and Brand24, we analyse audience sentiment, track competitor activity, and surface emerging trends across platforms. These insights guide content themes, messaging angles, and campaign timing so every post aligns with what your audience is actively engaging with.

AI-Accelerated Content Ideation & Production

Great social content needs both creativity and structure. Our team leverages Claude and Gemini to generate high-quality content directions, caption variations, and messaging frameworks, while Napkin and Notion transform those ideas into organized content plans. Visual assets are developed using Freepik, Envato, and Canva AI, while video content is generated through Kling AI and refined with Descript. Publishing and scheduling run through Social Pilot and Pomelli, with ClickUp and Frame ensuring the entire process stays coordinated from concept to live post.
