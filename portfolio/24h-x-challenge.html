24h - X Challenge

VIDEO

The 24-hour challenge, inspired by military training, pushes participants to their physical and mental limits. The experience includes walking 45 km with a 25 kg backpack, climbing, rafting, and various other demanding activities. This extreme environment fosters valuable skills such as teamwork, discipline, and resilience. Documenting this event was an intense undertaking. Our team had to carry video equipment over 20 km while navigating the same tough conditions. Additionally, we managed all this on just 3 hours of sleep. The result, however, is a raw and authentic portrayal of human endurance and camaraderie.
