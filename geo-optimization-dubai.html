The New Search

What is Generative
Engine Optimization?

The way people search is fundamentally changing. Instead of typing keywords into Google, millions of users in the UAE and worldwide now ask ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Claude for recommendations, reviews, and answers. These AI tools don't show a list of blue links — they give one direct answer.

Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) is the discipline of ensuring your brand is the one AI recommends. Traditional SEO alone is no longer enough. AI search engines pull from different data sources, interpret content differently, and prioritize structured, authoritative information over keyword density.

If someone asks ChatGPT "What's the best marketing agency in Dubai?" — GEO determines whether your brand appears in the answer or your competitor's does.

AI search engines don't just crawl your content — they interpret it. The content that gets cited by ChatGPT and Perplexity shares common traits: it's authoritative, data-rich, clearly structured, and answers specific questions directly.

Our GEO content team restructures your existing content and creates new assets specifically designed to be extracted and cited by AI models. Technical Foundation

Technical AI Search
Foundation

Before AI can recommend you, it needs to be able to find and crawl your content. Most UAE websites actively block AI crawlers in their robots.txt without realizing it — making their brand invisible to ChatGPT, Perplexity, and other AI tools.

Dubai Prod's technical GEO setup ensures your website is fully accessible to every major AI crawler. We configure robots.txt permissions for GPTBot, PerplexityBot, ClaudeBot, and Google-Extended. We create and maintain your llms.txt file. We set up Bing Webmaster Tools for maximum ChatGPT visibility. And we implement the enhanced schema markup that AI systems use to extract structured information about your business.
