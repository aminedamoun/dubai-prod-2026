Dubai Prod Launches GEO & AI Search Optimization Service for UAE Brands

DP
Dubai Prod Team
April 1, 2026 6 min read

Dubai Prod today announced the launch of its Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) service, a specialized offering designed to help UAE businesses get found, cited, and recommended by AI search platforms including ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google Gemini, and Claude.

The launch responds to a fundamental shift in how consumers and businesses discover information online. Industry data indicates that over 40% of online searches now involve AI-powered tools, with users increasingly asking ChatGPT and similar platforms for direct recommendations rather than browsing traditional search engine results. Unlike Google, which returns a list of links, AI search engines provide a single synthesized answer — meaning brands that are not optimized for AI visibility risk being excluded entirely from these conversations.

"The rules of search have changed overnight. When someone in Dubai asks ChatGPT 'What is the best marketing agency for my startup?' or 'Which restaurant should I visit in JBR?', the AI gives one answer. If your brand is not part of that answer, you do not exist to a rapidly growing segment of consumers. Traditional SEO alone is no longer sufficient."

Key Components of the GEO Service

  • •llms.txt file creation & optimization — A new web standard that helps AI crawlers understand your website
  • •AI crawler configuration — robots.txt setup for GPTBot, ChatGPT-User, PerplexityBot, ClaudeBot, Google-Extended
  • •Bing Webmaster optimization — Critical since ChatGPT Search is powered by Bing's index
  • •Enhanced schema markup — Structured data for AI content extraction including FAQPage, Organization, LocalBusiness
  • •AI-ready content restructuring — Question-answer formats, entity-based architecture, authoritative data-rich content
  • •Authority building — Strategic directory listings, citations, and guest placements for AI recommendation

Why GEO Matters for the UAE Market

The UAE's national AI strategy, combined with 98.6% social media penetration and the fastest AI adoption rate in the MENA region, makes this market uniquely positioned for GEO disruption. Consumers and business decision-makers in the UAE are among the earliest adopters of AI-powered search tools globally. Brands that move first on GEO optimization will secure a massive competitive advantage, as AI search engines tend to reinforce early authority signals — meaning the gap between optimized and non-optimized brands will only widen over time.

"We have already implemented GEO strategies for our own digital presence and seen measurable results. Our website now appears in AI-generated recommendations for key queries related to marketing agencies in Dubai. The businesses that move first on GEO will dominate AI search results in their verticals for years to come."

Availability

The GEO & AI Search Optimization service is available immediately as a standalone engagement or as an add-on to existing Dubai Prod marketing packages. Tiered packages include GEO Audit, Full GEO Implementation, AI Content Strategy, and Technical GEO Setup, designed for businesses of all sizes across the UAE.

Learn more about our GEO capabilities on our GEO & AI Search Optimization service page, or read our comprehensive GEO Optimization Guide for an in-depth look at how Generative Engine Optimization works.
