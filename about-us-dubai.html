Who We Are

Built for Bold
Brands

We don't just execute campaigns — we build movements.

Dubai Prod was founded in 2019 with a singular vision: to build a creative agency that truly understands Dubai's fast-moving business landscape. What began as a small team of passionate creatives has grown into a full-service powerhouse with over 50 specialists across content, strategy, technology, and media.

We bridge culture, creativity, and commerce for brands that want to lead, not follow. From ambitious startups to established enterprise names, we've helped 249+ companies build their presence across the UAE, Saudi Arabia, and the broader MENA region.

Our approach is simple: understand your brand deeply, craft strategy that actually fits your market, and execute with relentless precision. Every campaign, every piece of content, every media placement — it all serves a singular goal: real, measurable growth for your business.
