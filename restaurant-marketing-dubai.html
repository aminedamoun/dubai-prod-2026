FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How much does restaurant marketing cost in Dubai?

Restaurant marketing budgets in Dubai typically range from AED 5,000 to AED 25,000 per month depending on scope. A basic package covering social media management and content creation starts around AED 5,000-8,000/month, while comprehensive packages including influencer marketing, paid ads, SEO, and food photography range from AED 15,000-25,000+ monthly. We tailor packages to your restaurant's size, goals, and competitive landscape.

Which social media platforms work best for restaurants?

Instagram and TikTok are the top-performing platforms for restaurants in Dubai. Instagram excels with food photography, Stories, and Reels that showcase dishes and ambiance. TikTok drives massive organic reach with behind-the-scenes kitchen content and viral food videos. Google Business Profile is essential for local discovery, while Facebook remains effective for event promotion and community building.

How can I get more customers from Google for my restaurant?

Start by optimizing your Google Business Profile with professional photos, accurate hours, and menu links. Respond to every review. Target local SEO keywords like "best Italian restaurant Dubai Marina" to rank in the local 3-pack. Run Google Ads with location targeting and call extensions. We also optimize your Google Maps presence to capture "restaurants near me" searches.

Do you handle food photography and content creation?

Yes, Dubai Prod provides end-to-end content creation including professional food photography, short-form video production (Reels and TikToks), menu photography, interior and ambiance shots, chef portraits, and behind-the-scenes content. Our food photographers understand lighting, plating aesthetics, and the visual language that drives engagement on social media.

How long before I see results from restaurant marketing?

Social media growth becomes visible within 4-6 weeks. Influencer campaigns drive immediate foot traffic within days. Google Ads generate calls and reservations from day one. SEO takes 3-6 months but delivers compounding returns. Most restaurant clients see measurable increases in reservations and foot traffic within the first 60-90 days of a comprehensive campaign.

Can you help with restaurant launch marketing?

Absolutely. Restaurant launch marketing is one of our specialties. We create pre-launch buzz through teaser campaigns, influencer seeding, and PR outreach. Launch events include targeted influencer dinners, media tastings, and social media countdowns. Post-launch, we maintain momentum with ongoing content and paid campaigns. Our launch packages have helped restaurants achieve 1,000+ followers before opening day.
