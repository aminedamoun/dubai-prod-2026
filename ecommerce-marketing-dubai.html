// FAQ

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How much does e-commerce marketing cost in Dubai?

E-commerce marketing budgets in Dubai typically range from AED 5,000 to AED 50,000+ per month depending on your store size, product catalogue, and growth goals. This includes ad spend, platform management, and creative production. We tailor packages based on your revenue targets.

What are the best marketing platforms for e-commerce in Dubai?

Google Shopping and Performance Max drive the highest intent traffic. Meta Ads excel at dynamic product retargeting and prospecting. TikTok is growing fast for fashion, beauty, and lifestyle brands. Email marketing through Klaviyo delivers the best ROI for retention. We recommend a multi-channel approach.

How can I increase my online store's ROAS?

Improving ROAS requires optimizing product feed quality, refining audience targeting with lookalike models, running dynamic creative tests, improving landing page conversion rates, and reallocating budget to top-performing campaigns. Our clients see 40-80% ROAS improvements within 90 days.

Shopify vs custom e-commerce: which is better for marketing?

Shopify offers superior marketing integrations out of the box: native Google Shopping feeds, Meta Pixel, TikTok integration, and thousands of marketing apps. Custom platforms offer more flexibility but need more development for tracking. We work with both, but Shopify and WooCommerce accelerate results faster.

How long does it take to see results from e-commerce marketing?

Paid ads generate sales within the first week. Optimal performance takes 4-8 weeks of testing and optimization. SEO takes 3-6 months for meaningful organic traffic. Email flows show ROI within the first month. We recommend a minimum 3-month commitment for measurable, compounding results.

How can my online store compete with Amazon.ae and Noon?

D2C brands win by building direct customer relationships, offering unique brand experiences, and owning their data. We help stores build awareness through social, capture high-intent searches via Google Shopping, develop loyalty through email, and create content marketplaces cannot replicate. Many D2C clients outperform their marketplace channels in profitability.
