// FAQ

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How much does real estate marketing cost in Dubai?

Budgets typically range from AED 10,000 to AED 50,000+ per month depending on project scale. Off-plan launches for major developers often require AED 50,000-200,000+ for integrated campaigns. Boutique brokerages can start with AED 8,000-15,000/month for targeted digital campaigns.

What are the best platforms for real estate marketing in Dubai?

Instagram and Facebook lead for property marketing due to visual targeting. Google Ads captures high-intent buyers searching for specific communities. LinkedIn is key for B2B developer relationships. TikTok is growing for off-plan showcases targeting younger investors. A multi-platform strategy delivers the best results.

How do you generate qualified real estate leads online?

We combine targeted Meta Ads with lead forms, Google Search campaigns for high-intent property queries, SEO-optimized landing pages, virtual tour content that pre-qualifies buyers, and retargeting campaigns. Lead qualification includes budget verification, timeline assessment, and nationality targeting.

How long does it take to see results from real estate digital marketing?

Paid campaigns generate property inquiries within the first week. SEO for competitive real estate keywords takes 3-6 months. Social media brand building shows growth in 4-6 weeks. For off-plan launches, start marketing 8-12 weeks before launch date to build a qualified pipeline.

How do you market off-plan properties in Dubai?

Off-plan marketing uses a phased approach: pre-launch teasers, launch-day media blitzes with PR coverage, and sustained lead generation. We use 3D renders, virtual walkthroughs, drone footage, payment plan calculators, and developer credibility content to convert interest into reservations.

What ROI can I expect from real estate marketing?

Our real estate clients typically see 6-12x return on marketing investment. For a single off-plan unit sale worth AED 1-3M, marketing cost per acquisition ranges from AED 2,000-8,000. Top campaigns achieve 9.2x ROAS on Meta Ads and generate AED 14M+ in qualified pipeline from social media.
