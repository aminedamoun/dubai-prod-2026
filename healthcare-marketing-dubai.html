FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How much does healthcare marketing cost in Dubai?

Healthcare marketing budgets in Dubai typically range from AED 5,000 to AED 30,000 per month depending on your practice size. Single-location clinics can start with AED 5,000-10,000 monthly for SEO and Google Ads, while multi-branch hospitals may invest AED 30,000+ for comprehensive campaigns. We tailor packages to your patient acquisition targets and specialty.

What are the best marketing platforms for doctors?

Google Ads and Google Business Profile are the most effective for capturing high-intent patients. Instagram works well for cosmetic and dental practices with visual results. LinkedIn is ideal for specialist doctor branding and professional networking. We also leverage health directories like Practo for UAE-specific patient reach.

What patient acquisition strategies work best for clinics?

The most effective strategies combine local SEO for "dentist near me" searches, targeted Google Ads for specific treatments, a strong Google Reviews profile (4.5+ stars), educational content marketing, and social media showcasing patient results. We build multi-channel funnels that nurture patients from search to booked appointment.

How do you handle compliance in medical marketing?

We follow DHA (Dubai Health Authority) and HAAD advertising guidelines strictly. All claims are evidence-based, patient testimonials comply with consent regulations, before-and-after images follow approved formats, and we avoid prohibited claims. Our team is trained in UAE healthcare advertising regulations to protect your practice.

How long does it take to see results?

Google Ads can generate patient inquiries within the first week. SEO results typically take 3-6 months for first-page rankings on competitive medical keywords. Reputation management shows improvements within 2-3 months. Most clinics see a measurable increase in bookings within the first 60 days of a comprehensive strategy.

What ROI can I expect from medical marketing?

Healthcare marketing delivers strong ROI because patient lifetime value is high. Our clinic clients average 4-8x return on marketing spend. A single new patient from a Google Ad costing AED 50-150 can generate AED 2,000-50,000+ in treatment revenue. We track cost-per-patient-acquisition and revenue attribution to optimize your return continuously.
