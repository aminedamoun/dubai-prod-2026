FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What does a PR agency in Dubai do?

A PR agency in Dubai manages your public image through media relations, press releases, crisis communications, event PR, influencer partnerships, and thought leadership positioning. We secure coverage in outlets like Gulf News, Arabian Business, Forbes Middle East, and international media.

How much does PR cost in Dubai?

PR retainers in Dubai range from AED 5,000-25,000/month depending on scope and media targets. Project-based PR campaigns start from AED 15,000. We offer transparent pricing with clear deliverables including guaranteed media pitches, press releases, and monthly reporting.

How to get featured in Gulf News or Forbes Middle East?

Getting featured requires newsworthy angles, strong media relationships, and professional press materials. We craft compelling narratives, maintain relationships with 200+ journalists across the region, and pitch strategically to maximize your chances of premium coverage.

What is crisis PR management?

Crisis PR is rapid-response reputation management during negative events — bad press, social media backlash, legal issues, or operational failures. We provide 24/7 monitoring, stakeholder communication, media response strategies, and narrative recovery plans.

How long does a PR campaign take to show results?

PR results typically appear within 4-8 weeks for media placements. Building sustained brand authority and thought leadership takes 3-6 months of consistent PR activity. We track coverage, sentiment, reach, and brand mention growth monthly.
